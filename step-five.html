Step 5 - The Blog

Published: Tue 10 October 2023 | Tags: programming, pelican, pelican tutorial, python,

Blogs can be a great way to share your interest, knowledge or when you travel, you can share your adventure in a blog.

The Python syntax is super easy to read and follow. Pelican is also very simple and ideal for you as an introduction to programming. This tutorial will help you set up a blog, a website or both depending on what need is. And host it on GitHub for the world to see.

Setting the blog up is done a just a few steps, the hard part is to consistently write blog post, week end and week out.

Upload Images for the first page

If you are following along, download these two images and drop them in the images folder strawberries image and strawberries and yogurt image.

Setting Up Blog

  1. cd in and cd .. out of folders, till you are in content.
  2. mkdir Blog or mkdir My Yogurt Journey or whatever name is approprite for your subject matter.
  3. cd Blog
  4. touch firstpost.md
  5. Type the following in the file
1 Title: Strawberries
2 Date: 2023-10-01
3 Category: My Yogurt Journey
4 Slug: strawberries
